Occupational Therapy

Bachelor of Science [Occupational Therapy]

OTstands for Occupational Therapy ,it is the branch of science that deals with curing and rehabilitating the physically, emotionally and mentally disabled and challenged people. It focuses on adapting the environment according to the patient, and teaching and educating them in the necessary skills needed in performing daily activities. This was also known as ‘Moral Treatment’ in the past.
